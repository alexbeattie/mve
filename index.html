Malibu Valley Estates

M alibu Valley balances luxurious living with nature. Located on famed Mulholland Highway , across from Malibu Creek State Park and King Gillette Ranch in the Santa Monica Mountains, Malibu Valley includes 26 homes situated on approximately 100 acres, offering homeowners privacy, space, and community.

Each home in Malibu Valley Estates is a distinct architectural style combining timeless design with thoughtfully planned custom finishes throughout.

View Site Plan

Location

L ocated less than 5-miles from Malibu beaches and adjacent to over 8,000 acres of natural habitat and wilderness, Malibu Valley is situated in the Santa Monica Mountains National Recreation Area, which boasts more than 153,000 acres designated for outdoor recreation.

The Southern California lifestyle offers world class beaches, hiking, mountain biking, and horseback riding, along with sporting venues, theater, music concerts and fine dining. Everything is within reach at Malibu Valley.
